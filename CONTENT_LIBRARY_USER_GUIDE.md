# Content Library - User Guide

## What is the Content Library?

The Content Library is your personal collection of reusable content snippets. Store blogs, topics, paragraphs, code snippets, quotes, and more - then quickly search and copy them whenever you need them!

## Getting Started

### Opening the Content Library

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Look for the **"Content Library"** button in the top-right area
3. Click it to open the side panel

### Closing the Content Library

- Click the **X** button in the top-right corner of the panel
- Or click the **"Content Library"** button again

---

## Adding Content Snippets

### Step-by-Step

1. **Open the Content Library** (see above)
2. Click the **"+ Add"** button at the top
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Title**: Give your snippet a descriptive name
   - **Category**: Choose what type of content it is
   - **Content**: Paste or type your content
4. Click **"Add Snippet"**

### Example

**Title**: "Welcome Email Template"  
**Category**: Text  
**Content**: 
```
Dear [Name],

Welcome to our platform! We're excited to have you here.

Best regards,
The Team
```

## Finding Your Snippets

### Search

1. Type in the **search box** at the top of the panel
2. Results appear instantly as you type
3. Search looks in both **titles** and **content**

**Example**: Type "email" to find all email-related snippets

### Filter by Category

1. Click the **category dropdown** (shows "All Categories" by default)
2. Select a specific category to see only those snippets
3. Select "All Categories" to see everything again

### Combine Search + Filter

You can use both at the same time!
- **Example**: Filter by "Code" category, then search for "react"

## Copying Content

### Quick Copy

1. Find the snippet you want
2. Click the **copy icon** (üìã) on the right side
3. The icon changes to a **checkmark** (‚úì) for 2 seconds
4. You'll see a notification: "Copied to clipboard"
5. **Paste** anywhere using:
   - **Windows/Linux**: Ctrl+V
   - **Mac**: Cmd+V

### What Gets Copied?

The **entire content** of the snippet is copied as plain text, preserving line breaks and formatting.

## Tips & Tricks

### 1. Use Descriptive Titles

**Good**: "React useState Hook Example"  
**Bad**: "Code 1"

Descriptive titles make searching easier!

### 2. Organize with Categories

Keep similar content together by using the right category. This makes filtering much faster.

### 3. Keep Content Concise

Snippets work best when they're focused and reusable. Break large content into multiple snippets.

### 4. Use Search Effectively

- Search for **keywords** that appear in your content
- Try different search terms if you don't find what you're looking for
- Remember: search looks in both title AND content

### 5. Create Templates

Store templates for common tasks:
- Email responses
- Meeting notes
- Code boilerplates
- Standard paragraphs

### 6. Regular Cleanup

Periodically review and delete snippets you no longer use to keep your library organized.

## Troubleshooting

### "No snippets yet"

This means you haven't added any snippets yet. Click "+ Add" to create your first one!

### Search returns no results

- Check your spelling
- Try a different search term
- Make sure you're not filtering by the wrong category
- Clear the search box to see all snippets

### Copy button doesn't work

- Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Check that you're on a secure connection (HTTPS)
- Try refreshing the page
- Some browsers require clipboard permissions

### Panel won't open

- Try refreshing the page
- Make sure you're logged in
- Check your internet connection

## Privacy & Security

### Your Data is Private

- Only **you** can see your snippets
- Snippets are stored securely in the database
- No one else (including admins) can access your content library

### Data Storage

- Snippets are stored in your account
- They persist across devices when you log in
- Deleting your account will delete all your snippets

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many snippets can I store?

There's no hard limit! Store as many as you need.

### Can I share snippets with others?

Not yet, but this feature may be added in the future.

### Can I organize snippets into folders?

Currently, you can organize by categories. Folders may be added in future updates.

### Can I export my snippets?

Not yet, but import/export functionality may be added in the future.

### Do snippets sync across devices?

Yes! Your snippets are stored in your account, so they're available on any device where you log in.

### Can I add images to snippets?

Currently, snippets support text only. Image support may be added in the future.

### Is there a mobile app?

The Content Library works in your mobile browser! Just log in to the web app.
