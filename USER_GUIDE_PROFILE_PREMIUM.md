## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Profile Picture Upload

**Q: Why is my image being compressed?**
A: Images larger than 1MB are automatically compressed to ensure fast loading times and reasonable storage usage. The compression maintains good quality while reducing file size.

**Q: Can I upload a non-square image?**
A: Yes! The image will be displayed in a circular frame, so the center of your image will be visible. For best results, use a square image.

**Q: How do I delete my profile picture?**
A: Upload a new picture to replace the old one. The old picture is automatically deleted when you upload a new one.

**Q: What if my upload fails?**
A: Check that:
- Your file is in a supported format
- Your internet connection is stable
- The file isn't corrupted
- Try a different image if the problem persists
