# Quick Start Guide: Sharing Documents with Invitation Links

## For Document Owners

### Step 1: Open Your Document
- Navigate to Dashboard
- Click on the document you want to share

### Step 2: Generate Invitation Link
1. Click the **"Share"** button in the toolbar
2. In the Share dialog, configure:
   - **Access Role**: Choose "Editor" or "Viewer"
   - **Expires In**: Enter number of days (e.g., 7)
   - **Max Uses**: Leave empty for unlimited, or enter a number
3. Click **"Generate Invitation Link"**

### Step 3: Copy and Share
1. Find your new link in the "Active Invitation Links" section
2. Click the **copy icon** next to the link
3. Share the copied URL with your collaborators via:
   - Email
   - Chat (Slack, Teams, etc.)
   - Social media
   - Any messaging platform

### Step 4: Manage Links (Optional)
- View usage statistics (how many times used)
- See expiration dates
- Delete links you no longer need (trash icon)

## For Recipients

### Step 1: Click the Invitation Link
- You'll receive a link like: `https://app.com/invite/abc-123`
- Click the link to open the invitation page

### Step 2: Review Invitation Details
You'll see:
- Document title
- Who invited you
- Your assigned role (Editor or Viewer)
- What permissions you'll have
- Expiration date (if any)

### Step 3: Accept or Decline

**If you're already logged in:**
- Click **"Accept Invitation"** to join
- Click **"Decline"** if you don't want access

**If you're not logged in:**
- Click **"Login to Accept"**
- Sign in or create an account
- You'll be redirected back to accept the invitation

### Step 4: Start Collaborating
- After accepting, you'll be taken directly to the document
- Start viewing or editing based on your role

## Tips & Tricks

### For Owners
- **Set expiration dates** for temporary access
- **Use max uses** to limit the number of collaborators
- **Delete old links** to maintain security
- **Use Viewer role** for people who only need to read
- **Use Editor role** for active collaborators

### For Recipients
- **Check the source** before accepting invitations
- **Review permissions** to understand what you can do
- **Create an account** before clicking invitation links
- **Decline unwanted invitations** to keep your workspace clean

## Troubleshooting

### "Invitation not found or has expired"
- The link may have expired
- The owner may have deleted the invitation
- Check if you have the complete URL

### "This invitation has reached its maximum number of uses"
- Too many people have used the link
- Ask the owner to create a new invitation link

### "You have already accepted this invitation"
- You're already a collaborator on this document
- Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Shared Documents to find it

### "Failed to accept invitation"
- You might already be a collaborator
- The invitation might have expired
- Try refreshing the page and accepting again
